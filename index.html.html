AboutUs

Reteng Wellness Centre is a fully registered Non-Profit Organization (NPO)operating in Kimberley.
Reteng Wellness Centre was founded in 2019 and hasbeen in existence for a year and few months, but
its founding members havebeen for years been supporting NPOs offering the same courses focusing
oncommunity uplifments and GBV and outreach projects (Sanitary Pads, DignityPacks - Toiletry, School
Uniforms and Clothes for the needy)
